Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

FreeGenAI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our free AI image generation tool and how we use it.

FreeGenAI does not require account creation to use the core image generation features. We do not collect personal information such as your name, address, or phone number unless you voluntarily provide it (for example, if you choose to sign up for an account in the future).

Text prompts you enter are sent to our AI image generation provider (Pollinations.ai) to create images. We do not store your prompts or generated images on our own servers.
